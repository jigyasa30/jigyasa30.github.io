Initial Response (IR)

Once the case is created, it is assigned to the Smart Tier-1 team. The smart T1 engineer sends the Initial response to the customer acknowledging that the ticket has been received and is under analysis.


After sending IR to the customer, smart tier-1 engineer assigns this case to the concerned support team for analysis
